LYING TRICEPS EXTENSION

How to do it:
1. Starting position for this practical triceps exercise is lying on a standard bench holding a barbell in both hands with an overhand grip, hands at shoulder width apart.
2. Begin with arms over your upper chest and elbows bent back at about a 45-degree angle.
3. First bend at the elbows and then allow the upper arm to drop back, bringing the barbell behind your head and down toward the floor. In terms of upper arm position, the barbell should never be fully above your head, but instead behind it, to ensure that youâ€™re targeting the triceps.
4. Keep the shoulder blades tucked under, the elbows tight in toward your head and your core active during this entire movement.

3-4 sets of 8-12 reps

TRICEP PUSHDOWN

How to do it:
1. Starting position for this versatile triceps exercise is standing feet flat with feet shoulder width apart facing a cable pulley machine with a short straight bar attachment.
2. Begin to pull the bar attachment down toward your legs and ensure that you straighten your elbows until lockout before returning to the original position at chest level.



3-4 sets of 8-12 reps

DIAMOND PUSH-UP

How to do it:
1. For this body weight Diamond Push-Up, starting position is in high plank position with hands on the floor in a perfect diamond shape toward midline of the chest with thumbs touching each other.
2. Lower down completely before pushing back up to the starting position of the Diamond Push-Up.
3. Maintain core strength throughout the exercise. The elbow position remains close to the body.


3-4 sets of 8-12 reps

CLOSE GRIP PIN PRESS

How to do it:
1. Start position for this close-grip variation is lying on a bench with the barbell positioned across the pins in rack position, hands shoulder width apart and palms facing away from you gripping the bar.
2. Slowly lower the weight load, keeping elbows tucked in close to your side until the pins stop you, and then return to the starting position.
3. Keep the shoulder blades tucked under and maintain core strength throughout the exercise.


3-4 sets of 8-12 reps

CABLE TRICEPS PUSHAWAY

How to do it:
1. Start with feet in a staggered stance facing away from a cable station with upper arm position above the head, elbows bent, and hands gripping both handles of a rope attachment.
2. With your upper body angled slightly toward the floor, begin to push the cable attachments outward, extending the elbows completely.
3. On the way back, allow the arms to stretch backward behind the head before returning to neutral position.
4. Maintain core stability throughout the movement.
5. Transition immediately into the Drag Pushdown.

3-4 sets of 8-12 reps

Underhand Kickback

How to do it:
1. Stand holding a dumbbell in each hand. Hinge at your hips to lower your torso until it's almost parallel to the floor.
2. Turn your palms to face out, elbows slightly bent.
3. Keep your upper arms against your sides as you extend your elbows until your arms are parallel to your torso.
4. Reverse the motion to return to the start position.


3-4 sets of 8-12 reps

Dip

How to do it:
1. Use dip bars if available, or place your palms on a bench or chair, and extend your legs in front of you (shown).
2. Lower your body until your upper arms are parallel to the floor but no lower.
3. Extend your elbows to come up.



3-4 sets of 8-12 reps
